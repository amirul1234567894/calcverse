Tipping Guide for India

Tipping culture in India is different from Western countries. While tips are appreciated, they are generally not mandatory. Here is a quick guide.

  • Restaurants: 10% for good service, 15% for excellent service
  • Hotel Staff: Rs. 50-100 per bag for bellhop, Rs. 100-200 per day for housekeeping
  • Food Delivery: Rs. 20-50 per delivery
  • Cab or Auto: Round up the fare or add Rs. 10-20
  • Salon or Spa: 10-15% of service cost
