Common Unit Conversions

Our unit converter supports the most commonly used conversions: kilometers to miles, kilograms to pounds, Celsius to Fahrenheit, meters to feet, liters to gallons, and many more. The calculator covers metric (SI) and imperial (US/UK) systems.

Length Quick Reference

1 inch = 2.54 cm | 1 foot = 30.48 cm | 1 yard = 0.9144 m | 1 mile = 1.60934 km | 1 nautical mile = 1.852 km

Weight Quick Reference

1 pound = 0.453592 kg | 1 ounce = 28.3495 g | 1 stone = 6.35029 kg | 1 ton (short) = 907.185 kg

Temperature Reference

°F = °C × 9/5 + 32 | °C = (°F – 32) × 5/9 | K = °C + 273.15

FAQs

What is the difference between metric and imperial?

Metric (SI) uses meters, kilograms, litres. Imperial uses feet, pounds, gallons. Most countries use metric; the US primarily uses imperial.

How do I convert temperature?

Celsius to Fahrenheit: multiply by 9/5 and add 32. Celsius to Kelvin: add 273.15.
