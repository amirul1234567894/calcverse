Power of Compound Interest

Compound interest earns interest on both principal and accumulated interest, growing exponentially over time. Simple interest only earns on the original principal.

Example: Rs. 1 Lakh at 8% for 10 Years

  • Simple Interest: Rs. 1,80,000 - earn Rs. 80,000
  • Compound Yearly: Rs. 2,15,892 - earn Rs. 1,15,892
  • Compound Quarterly: Rs. 2,20,804 - earn Rs. 1,20,804
  • Compound Monthly: Rs. 2,22,039 - earn Rs. 1,22,039
