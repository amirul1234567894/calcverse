Indian Rupee Exchange Rates 2026

The Indian Rupee (INR) is managed by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). Exchange rates fluctuate daily based on trade, inflation, and monetary policy.

Popular Currency Pairs with INR

  • USD to INR: Most traded pair. 1 USD approximately 83 to 85 INR in 2026.
  • AED to INR: Important for NRIs in UAE. UAE Dirham is pegged to USD.
  • GBP to INR: For Indian students and professionals in UK.
  • SGD to INR: For Indian diaspora in Singapore.
  • EUR to INR: Key for trade with European Union.

Tips for Currency Exchange in India

  • Compare rates at multiple banks before exchanging
  • Authorized Money Changers often give better rates than banks
  • Avoid airport forex kiosks - they have the worst rates
  • Use forex cards for international travel for better rates
  • Above Rs. 7 lakh foreign remittance, TCS of 20% applies
