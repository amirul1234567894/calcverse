Your Daily Calorie Needs

How Many Calories Do You Need Per Day?

This calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, widely considered the most accurate formula for estimating Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). Your TDEE is BMR multiplied by your activity factor.

Calorie Goals by Objective

  • Weight Maintenance: Eat at your TDEE level
  • Gradual Weight Loss: TDEE minus 300 to 500 calories - lose 0.3 to 0.5 kg per week
  • Faster Weight Loss: TDEE minus 500 to 750 calories - lose 0.5 to 0.8 kg per week
  • Muscle Building: TDEE plus 200 to 400 calories with strength training

Indian Food Calorie Reference

  • 1 Roti or chapati: 80 calories
  • 1 cup cooked rice: 200 calories
  • Dal 1 cup: 150 calories
  • 1 Idli: 40 calories
  • 1 Samosa: 150 to 200 calories
  • Paneer 100g: 265 calories
