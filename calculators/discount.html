How to Calculate Discount

Discount calculation is simple: Discount Amount = Original Price multiplied by Discount Percentage divided by 100. Final Price = Original Price minus Discount Amount.

Discount Examples

  • 30% off Rs. 2000: Save Rs. 600, pay Rs. 1400
  • 50% off Rs. 5000: Save Rs. 2500, pay Rs. 2500
  • 10% off Rs. 999: Save Rs. 99.90, pay Rs. 899.10
  • 40% off Rs. 1499: Save Rs. 599.60, pay Rs. 899.40
