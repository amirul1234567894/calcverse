Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

CalcVerse does not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily submit it via our contact form. All calculator computations happen locally in your browser — no data is sent to our servers.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use our site. These tools may use cookies and collect anonymized usage data like page views and session duration.

3. Advertising

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality. Analytics and advertising cookies may be set by third-party services. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Your Data

All calculator inputs are processed locally in your browser. We do not store, log, or transmit any values you enter into our calculators.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, use our Contact page.
